<p><p>Deep-cycle batteries are primarily categorized into two types- flooded deep cycle batteries and sealed deep cycle batteries. Flooded deep cycle batteries are traditional batteries that require regular maintenance and refilling of electrolytes. On the other hand, sealed deep cycle batteries are maintenance-free and sealed, making them safer and easier to use. Both types are designed to provide sustained power over a long period of time, making them ideal for renewable energy storage, marine applications, RVs, and other off-grid power systems.</p></p>
