<p><p>Thermal circuit breakers can be classified into different types based on their mounting methods and applications. Integral type breakers are built into the equipment, while PCB mounting breakers are soldered onto printed circuit boards. Snap-in mounting breakers are easily attached to panels, while panel mounting breakers are fixed directly onto the panel surface. Plug-in type breakers are designed to be easily inserted and removed from sockets for quick installation and maintenance. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries and applications.</p></p>
