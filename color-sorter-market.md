<p><ul><li>Chute-Type Color Sorters</li><li>Belt-Type Color Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chute-Type Color Sorters are machines that use gravity to separate objects based on color. They have a simple design and are ideal for processing large quantities of products quickly. On the other hand, Belt-Type Color Sorters use a conveyor belt system to transport items past sensors that detect color differences and then divert them into separate containers. These machines are more suitable for fragile or delicate items that need gentle handling during the sorting process. Both types of color sorters are widely used in industries such as food processing, agriculture, and recycling.</p></p>
