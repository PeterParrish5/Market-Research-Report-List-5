<p><p>Hydrated silica is available in two main market types: granules and powder. Granules are larger particles of hydrated silica that are often used for industrial purposes such as for catalyst support or as a desiccant. On the other hand, hydrated silica powder consists of fine particles that are commonly used in the manufacturing of toothpaste, cosmetics, and abrasive cleaners. Both forms of hydrated silica offer unique properties and applications depending on the desired end-use.</p></p>
