<p><p>LiNbO3 is commonly used in two main market types - Acoustic Grade and Optical Grade. Acoustic Grade LiNbO3 is primarily used in surface acoustic wave devices in various applications such as sensors, filters, and delay lines. On the other hand, Optical Grade LiNbO3 is utilized in the telecommunications industry for modulators, switches, and frequency doublers due to its excellent electro-optic properties. Both grades cater to different industries and have specific applications that make them valuable in the market.</p></p>
