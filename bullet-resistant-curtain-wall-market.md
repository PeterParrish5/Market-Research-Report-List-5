<p><p>The bullet-resistant curtain wall market can be classified into two main types: unitized curtain wall and stick curtain wall. Unitized curtain walls are pre-assembled in factory-controlled environments and installed as large units on the building, providing efficient construction and high-quality finish. Stick curtain walls are installed piece by piece on-site, allowing for more customization and flexibility in design. Both types offer varying levels of protection against bullets and are commonly used in high-security buildings such as government facilities, banks, and airports.</p></p>
