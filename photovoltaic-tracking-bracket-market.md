<p><p>The photovoltaic tracking bracket market includes two main types: two-row component tracking and single-row component tracking. Two-row component tracking systems consist of two sets of photovoltaic panels that can move independently to track the sun's position. Single-row component tracking systems have a single row of panels that rotate together to follow the sun. Both types of tracking systems help maximize energy generation by ensuring that the panels are always facing the sun at the optimal angle for maximum sunlight exposure.</p></p>
