<p><p>Portable moisture analyzers come in various types, including microwave, infrared, loss on drying (LOD), and others. Microwave analyzers use microwave technology to measure moisture content, while infrared analyzers use infrared light to determine moisture levels. Loss on drying analyzers involve heating the sample to remove moisture and calculating the loss in weight. Other types of analyzers may use different technologies or methods to measure moisture content in a portable and convenient manner. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
