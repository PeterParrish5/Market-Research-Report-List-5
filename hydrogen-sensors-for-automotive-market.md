Pressure sensors monitor hydrogen pressure in fuel systems, ensuring optimal performance. Temperature sensors measure temperature changes to prevent overheating in hydrogen-powered vehicles. Hydrogen exhaust sensors detect hydrogen leaks, improving safety. Mass air flow sensors measure the flow of air and hydrogen mixtures for efficient combustion. These sensors play a crucial role in maintaining the performance and safety of hydrogen-powered vehicles.</p></p>
