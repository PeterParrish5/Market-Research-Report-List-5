<p><p>Fire suppression systems for mining come in three main types: dry chemicals, liquid chemicals, and dual agent systems. Dry chemical systems release powder to suppress fires, while liquid chemical systems use water or foam. Dual agent systems combine dry chemicals and liquid chemicals for maximum effectiveness. Each system has its own advantages and is used in different mining environments depending on factors such as the type of materials being mined and the size of the operation.</p></p>
