<p><p>Current Mode PWM Controllers are a type of programmable PWM controller that regulates the output by monitoring the current flowing through the power switch. This allows for better control of the power supply and improved stability. </p><p>Voltage Mode PWM Controllers, on the other hand, regulate the output by monitoring the output voltage instead of the current. This type of controller offers precise voltage regulation and is widely used in various applications such as power supplies, motor control, and lighting. Both types play a crucial role in the programmable PWM controllers market, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
