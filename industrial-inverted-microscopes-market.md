<p><ul><li>Episcopic Type</li><li>Diascopic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial inverted microscopes are commonly categorized into two types: episcopic and diascopic. Episcopic type microscopes illuminate the specimen from above, allowing for observation of opaque samples such as metals or ceramics. Diascopic type microscopes, on the other hand, transmit light through the specimen from below, making them suitable for transparent samples like biological specimens. Both types of microscopes are widely used in various industries for a range of applications, including research, quality control, and inspection.</p></p>
