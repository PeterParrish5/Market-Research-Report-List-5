<p><p>Incremental rotary encoders are devices used to convert rotary motion into electrical signals. The three main types of incremental rotary encoders are mechanical, optical, and magnetic. Mechanical encoders use a metal disc with slots that interrupt a light beam to generate pulses. Optical encoders utilize a light source and photoelectric sensor to produce pulses. Magnetic encoders use magnets and Hall effect sensors to detect changes in magnetic fields. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in various industries such as automation, robotics, and machinery.</p></p>
