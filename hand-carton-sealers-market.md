<p><p>Hand Carton Sealers market consists of three main types: Manual Carton Sealers, Fully Automatic Carton Sealers, and Semi-Automatic Carton Sealers. Fully Automatic Carton Sealers, on the other hand, can handle high-volume operations with minimal human intervention. Semi-Automatic Carton Sealers fall in between these two, requiring some manual input while also featuring automated sealing functions. The choice of sealer type depends on the volume and speed of carton sealing operations.</p></p>
