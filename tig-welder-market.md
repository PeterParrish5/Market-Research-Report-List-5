<p><p>TIG welder market is divided into different types based on the technology used. Pulsed-current TIG welders provide precise control over the welding process, allowing for increased control over heat input and weld penetration. This makes them ideal for thin materials and intricate welds. On the other hand, dabber TIG welders are designed for high deposition rates, making them suitable for welding thicker materials and creating strong welds. Both types cater to the specific needs of different welding applications in various industries.</p></p>
