<p><p>The submergible mixer market can be categorized into two main types based on the power supply: Monophase and Three-phase mixers. Monophase mixers operate on a single-phase electrical power supply, which is common in residential and small-scale industrial applications. On the other hand, Three-phase mixers require a three-phase electrical power supply and are typically used in larger industrial settings where higher power requirements are needed. Both types of mixers serve the purpose of agitating and mixing liquids efficiently in various industries such as wastewater treatment, food processing, and chemical production.</p></p>
