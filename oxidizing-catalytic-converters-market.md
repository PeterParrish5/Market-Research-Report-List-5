Three-way converters are designed to reduce three harmful pollutants (hydrocarbons, carbon monoxide, and nitrogen oxides) into less harmful substances. Two-way converters, on the other hand, are designed to reduce hydrocarbons and carbon monoxide. Both types play a significant role in ensuring cleaner air and reducing the environmental impact of vehicle emissions.</p></p>
