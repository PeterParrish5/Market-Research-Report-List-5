<p><p>Resistance Furnace Power Transformers are classified into Direct Voltage Regulation and Indirect Voltage Regulation market types based on their regulation methods. In Direct Voltage Regulation, the voltage is adjusted directly to the required level using taps on the primary winding. In contrast, Indirect Voltage Regulation utilizes an external device to control the output voltage indirectly. Both market types cater to different preferences and requirements of customers in terms of voltage regulation methods for resistance furnace power transformers.</p></p>
