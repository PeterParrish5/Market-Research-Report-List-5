<p><strong>The Automotive Hub Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hub motor market consists of two main types: inner-rotor motors and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors have the stator on the outside and the rotor on the inside, resulting in a compact design that is suitable for high-speed applications. External-rotor motors have the stator on the inside and the rotor on the outside, offering better heat dissipation and torque characteristics. Both types of hub motors have their unique advantages and are used in various automotive applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
