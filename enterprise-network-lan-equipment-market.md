<p><ul><li>Token Ring</li><li>Fiber Distributed Data Interface (FDDI)</li><li>ARCNET</li><li>Ethernet</li><li>Wireless LAN (WLAN) or Wi-Fi</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise Network LAN Equipment market consists of various types such as Token Ring, FDDI, ARCNET, Ethernet, and WLAN or Wi-Fi. Token Ring technology uses a ring topology to transmit data, while FDDI utilizes a dual ring topology for redundancy. ARCNET is a bus-based network architecture, Ethernet is the most commonly used LAN technology that uses a bus or star topology, and WLAN/Wi-Fi enables wireless connectivity. These different types of LAN equipment cater to various network requirements and offer different features and capabilities for efficient data transmission within an organization.</p></p>
