<p><strong>The MEO Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active MEO antennas are equipped with amplifiers or signal boosters to enhance signal strength and performance. They require external power source for operation. These antennas are suitable for long-distance communication and high-speed data transmission. On the other hand, passive MEO antennas do not require external power source and rely on natural signals for communication. They are simpler in design and cost-effective but may have limited range and slower data transmission speeds compared to active antennas. Both active and passive MEO antennas cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
