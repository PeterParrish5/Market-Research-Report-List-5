<p><p>Electrical cable conduits made of metal are available in two main types: flexible and rigid conduits. Flexible conduits are commonly used in applications where the wiring needs to be easily maneuvered and are made of corrugated metal or interlocking aluminum strips. On the other hand, rigid conduits are solid tubes made of steel, aluminum, or PVC and are used in applications where the wiring needs protection from physical damage or environmental factors. Both types serve the purpose of enclosing and protecting electrical cables.</p></p>
