<p><p>A Distributed Feedback Laser Diode (DFB-LD) is a type of semiconductor laser diode that features a grating structure on the laser cavity to provide wavelength-selective feedback. This allows for stable single-mode operation and narrow linewidth emission, making DFB-LDs ideal for applications requiring high precision and stability, such as optical communications, sensing, and metrology.</p><p>The Distributed Feedback Laser Diode (DFB-LD) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period.
