<p><p>Semiconductor test socket market types include BGA (Ball Grid Array), QFP (Quad Flat Package), QFN (Quad Flat No-Lead), LGA (Land Grid Array), SOIC (Small Outline Integrated Circuit), as well as others. Each type of test socket is designed to accommodate different types of semiconductor packages for testing purposes. BGA sockets have solder balls for electrical connection, while QFP sockets have leads on all four sides. QFN sockets have no leads, while LGA sockets have lands for connection. SOIC sockets have a small outline for easy installation. Other types of sockets cater to specific package designs.</p></p>
