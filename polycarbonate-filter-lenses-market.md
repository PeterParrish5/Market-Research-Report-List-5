<p><p>Polycarbonate filter lenses are available in three main types based on their light filtering capacities: weak filter, neutral filter, and strong filter. Weak filter lenses provide minimal protection and are best suited for low-light conditions. Neutral filter lenses offer moderate protection against glare and harmful UV rays, making them ideal for general use. Strong filter lenses offer maximum protection against intense sunlight and are recommended for outdoor activities in bright environments. Each type caters to different light conditions and user preferences.</p></p>
