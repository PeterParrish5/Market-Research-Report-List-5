<p><p>Lithography systems market consists of three main types: contact printers, proximity printers, and projection printers. Contact printers use direct contact between the mask and the substrate, proximity printers use a small gap between the mask and the substrate, and projection printers use a lens system to project the mask pattern onto the substrate. Each type offers different levels of resolution and accuracy, catering to the diverse needs of various industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, display technologies, and microelectronics.</p></p>
