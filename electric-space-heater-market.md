<p><p>Electric space heaters come in various types, including ceramic heaters, oil-filled heaters, infrared heaters, and others. Ceramic heaters use ceramic plates to generate heat quickly and efficiently. Oil-filled heaters use heated oil to provide a longer-lasting heat source. Infrared heaters use electromagnetic radiation to warm people and objects directly, without heating the surrounding air. Other types of electric space heaters include fan-forced heaters, radiant heaters, and convection heaters, each offering unique benefits and features for heating specific spaces.</p></p>
