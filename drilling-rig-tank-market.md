<p><ul><li>Closed</li><li>Open</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drilling rig tanks come in three main types: Closed, Open, and Others. Closed tanks are sealed units that prevent any outside contaminants from entering, making them ideal for storing sensitive materials. Open tanks are not sealed and allow for easy access to the contents, making them suitable for less critical materials. The Others category includes tanks with unique designs or features that do not fit into the Closed or Open categories. Each type of tank is used based on the specific needs and requirements of the drilling operation.</p></p>
