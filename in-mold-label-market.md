<p><p>In-mold labels can be categorized based on the type of resin used. Polypropylene in-mold labels offer high clarity and excellent resistance to chemicals and temperature changes. Polyethylene labels are known for their toughness and flexibility, making them ideal for curved surfaces. ABS resin labels are durable and impact-resistant, making them suitable for products that may undergo rough handling. Polyvinyl chloride labels are known for their rigidity and weather resistance, making them a popular choice for outdoor applications. Each type offers unique properties to cater to different labeling needs.</p></p>
