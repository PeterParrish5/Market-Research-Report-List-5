<p><ul><li>Chain Structure</li><li>Cyclic Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Organic aluminum can be classified into two main market types: chain structure and cyclic structure. In a chain structure market, aluminum compounds are linked together in a linear form, creating a long chain-like structure. On the other hand, in a cyclic structure market, aluminum compounds form closed-ring structures. Each structure type offers different properties and applications, with chain structures often being more flexible and cyclic structures being more rigid. Both types play a significant role in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
