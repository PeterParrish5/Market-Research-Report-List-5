<p><p>The Carrier Router Switch market consists of various types catering to different network needs. Multiservice Edge Router enables various services like voice, video, and data over a single network. ATM Switch enables fast data switching in Asynchronous Transfer Mode networks. Ethernet Service Edge Router provides connectivity and services at the network edge for Ethernet networks.</p></p>
