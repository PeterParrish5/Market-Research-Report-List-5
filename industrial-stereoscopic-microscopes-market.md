<p><p>Industrial stereoscopic microscopes are available in three main types based on the number of eyepieces they have: monocular, binocular, and trinocular. Monocular stereoscopic microscopes have a single eyepiece, making them more affordable and compact but limiting the viewing experience. Binocular stereoscopic microscopes have two eyepieces, providing a more comfortable and immersive viewing experience. Trinocular stereoscopic microscopes have three eyepieces, allowing for additional features like camera attachments for image capturing and sharing, making them ideal for research and documentation purposes.</p></p>
