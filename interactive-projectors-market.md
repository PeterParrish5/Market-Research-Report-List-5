<p><strong>The Interactive Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li><li>LCoS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interactive projectors utilize three main technologies in the market - DLP (Digital Light Processing), LCD (Liquid Crystal Display), and LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors use micro-mirrors to create images, providing high contrast and sharpness. LCD projectors use liquid crystal panels to produce images with vibrant colors and high brightness. LCoS projectors combine the benefits of both DLP and LCD technologies, offering high resolution and image quality. Each type of projector has its advantages and is used in various applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
