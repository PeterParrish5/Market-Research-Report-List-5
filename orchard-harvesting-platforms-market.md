<p><p>Orchard harvesting platforms can be categorized into two main types: self-propelled and towed platforms. Self-propelled platforms are equipped with their own engines and steering systems, allowing them to move independently through the orchard. Towed platforms, on the other hand, need to be towed by a tractor or other vehicle. The self-propelled platforms offer greater flexibility and maneuverability, while towed platforms are typically more cost-effective and suitable for smaller orchards. Both types of platforms play a crucial role in optimizing harvest operations in orchards.</p></p>
