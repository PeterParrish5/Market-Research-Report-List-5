<p><p>X-ray Particle Analyzer is a scientific instrument used to analyze the composition of materials. There are two main types of X-ray Particle Analyzers: X-ray Diffraction Type and X-ray Fluorescence Type. </p><p>X-ray Diffraction Type analyzes the crystalline structure of a material by measuring the angles and intensities of X-ray beams that are diffracted. </p><p>X-ray Fluorescence Type, on the other hand, analyzes the elemental composition of a material by measuring the fluorescent X-rays emitted when the material is exposed to high-energy X-rays. Both types of analyzers are crucial tools in various industries for materials analysis and quality control.</p></p>
