<p><p>Strip seal expansion joints are commonly utilized in infrastructure projects to accommodate movements in structures such as bridges. There are two main types available in the market: strip seal expansion joints with straight head and strip seal expansion joints with bending head. Strip seal expansion joints with straight head are typically used in straight sections of bridges, whereas strip seal expansion joints with bending head are designed for curved sections or where a change in direction is required. Both types provide effective protection against water infiltration and ensure smooth traffic flow.</p></p>
