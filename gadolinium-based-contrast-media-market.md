<p><p>Gadolinium-based contrast media market includes three main types: extracellular agents, blood pool agents, and hepatobiliary agents. Extracellular agents are commonly used for imaging of the blood vessels and organs outside of the central nervous system. Blood pool agents have longer circulation times in the blood, allowing for better visualization of the vascular system. Hepatobiliary agents are used specifically for imaging the liver and bile ducts. Each type of contrast media has specific characteristics that make them ideal for different imaging purposes.</p></p>
