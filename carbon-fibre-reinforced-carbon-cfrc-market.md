<p><p>Carbon Fibre Reinforced Carbon (CFRC) is a high-performance material used in various industries like aerospace and automotive. Two main types of CFRC production methods include Chemical Vapor Deposition and Liquid Impregnation Process. Chemical Vapor Deposition involves depositing carbon atoms onto a surface in a controlled environment, while Liquid Impregnation Process involves impregnating pre-formed carbon fiber with a liquid resin. These methods result in CFRC with superior strength, lightweight properties, and high temperature resistance.</p></p>
