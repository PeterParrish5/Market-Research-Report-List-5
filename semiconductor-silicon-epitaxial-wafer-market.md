<p><p>Semiconductor Silicon Epitaxial Wafer Market is categorized based on the type of dopants used in the silicon wafer. P-type wafer refers to a wafer where the silicon material is doped with elements that create a positive charge. N-type wafer refers to a wafer where the silicon material is doped with elements that create a negative charge. P-type wafers are used in devices such as transistors, while N-type wafers are used in devices such as diodes. Both types are essential components in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
