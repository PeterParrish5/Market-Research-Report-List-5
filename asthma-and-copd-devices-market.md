<p><p>Asthma and COPD devices market includes two main types: inhalers and nebulizers. Inhalers are handheld devices that deliver medication directly to the lungs through the mouth. They are portable and convenient for quick relief of symptoms. Nebulizers are larger devices that convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. They are often used for more severe cases or in young children who have difficulty using inhalers. Both types of devices play a crucial role in managing and controlling respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.</p></p>
