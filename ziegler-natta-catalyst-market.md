<p><p>Ziegler Natta Catalyst market comprises two main types: Metallocene Catalysts and Non-Metallocene Catalysts. Metallocene Catalysts are designed with a single catalytic site, offering high precision and control over polymer properties. They are known for producing polymers with uniform structures and improved performance. On the other hand, Non-Metallocene Catalysts consist of multiple catalytic sites, providing a higher degree of flexibility in polymer design. Both types play a crucial role in the polymer industry, catering to various applications and market demands.</p></p>
