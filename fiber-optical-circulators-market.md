<p><p>Fiber optic circulators are devices used in optical communication systems to control the direction of light transmission. They come in various configurations including 3-port and 4-port designs. In a 3-port circulator, light enters through one port and exits through the next port in a clockwise direction. A 4-port circulator allows light to enter through one port and exit through the next port in a counter-clockwise direction. These devices are essential for ensuring efficient and reliable data transmission in fiber optic networks.</p></p>
