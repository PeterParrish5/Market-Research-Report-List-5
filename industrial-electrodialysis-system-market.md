<p><p>An Industrial Electrodialysis System is a type of water treatment process that uses an electric current to remove impurities from water. <p><p>Industrial Electrodialysis System Market has two main types - Continuous Electrodialysis and Batch Electrodialysis. Continuous Electrodialysis operates continuously, allowing for a constant flow of ions through the system, making it suitable for large-scale industrial processes. On the other hand, Batch Electrodialysis operates in intervals, processing a finite amount of solution at a time. This type is often used for smaller-scale applications or in situations where precision and control over the process are required.</p></p>
