Pulse oximeters measure the oxygen saturation in the blood, while incentive spirometers help improve lung function through breathing exercises. Peak flow meters measure the speed at which air is exhaled from the lungs. Other respiratory monitors may include devices for monitoring respiratory rate, lung capacity, and breathing patterns.</p></p>
