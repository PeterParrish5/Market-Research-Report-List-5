ULPA filters are ultra-low particulate air filters that can remove 99.999% of particles larger than 0.12 microns. The "others" category includes filters such as medium-efficiency filters and pre-filters. Each type of filter is designed to meet specific cleanroom requirements based on the level of air purity needed.</p></p>
