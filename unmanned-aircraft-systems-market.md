<p><p>Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS) market consists of two main types: Fixed Wing and Rotary Wing. Fixed Wing UAS are designed with fixed wings similar to traditional airplanes, enabling them to fly longer distances and at higher speeds. They are ideal for surveillance, reconnaissance, and mapping missions. On the other hand, Rotary Wing UAS are equipped with rotary blades like helicopters, allowing them to hover and maneuver more easily in confined spaces. They are commonly used for tasks such as search and rescue, crop monitoring, and aerial photography.</p></p>
