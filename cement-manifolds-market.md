<p><strong>The Cement Manifolds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Plug</li><li>Double Plug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cement manifolds come in different types, including simple plug and double plug markets. In a simple plug market, a single plug is used to seal off one end of the manifold to control the flow of cement. On the other hand, a double plug market involves using two plugs to seal both ends of the manifold for more precise control over the cement flow. Each type offers its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the cementing operation.</p></p>
