<p><p>I-line photoresist refers to a type of photoresist material used in photolithography processes for semiconductor manufacturing. There are three main types of I-line photoresist materials: photopolymerization, light decomposition type, and photocrosslinked. Photopolymerization type uses UV light to induce a chemical reaction, while light decomposition type relies on light to break down the material. On the other hand, photocrosslinked type uses UV light to form crosslinks between molecules, creating a more stable and robust material for semiconductor production.</p></p>
