<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Light Imager Market includes two main types of image sensors: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD image sensors are known for their high-quality image output and low noise levels, making them ideal for low light conditions. On the other hand, CMOS image sensors are known for their low power consumption, fast readout speed, and cost-effective manufacturing process. Both CCD and CMOS sensors are widely used in applications such as surveillance cameras, automotive cameras, and medical imaging devices.</p></p>
