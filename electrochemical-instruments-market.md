<p><strong>The Electrochemical Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometry</li><li>Voltammetry</li><li>Coulometry</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrochemical instruments market is categorized into four main types: Potentiometry, Voltammetry, Coulometry, and Others. Potentiometry measures the voltage difference between two electrodes in a chemical system, indicating the concentration of ions. Voltammetry analyzes the current flow between electrodes to determine chemical species in a sample. Coulometry measures the total amount of electricity required to carry out a specific chemical reaction. The "Others" category includes various electrochemical techniques such as amperometry and electrochemical impedance spectroscopy.</p></p>
