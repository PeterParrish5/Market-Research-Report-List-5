<p><p>Transportation turnstile market includes two main types: Optical turnstiles and Arm turnstiles. Optical turnstiles use infrared beams to detect unauthorized entry and are popular in areas where aesthetics are important. They provide a sleek and modern look while ensuring security. Arm turnstiles, on the other hand, use a rotating arm to allow or block entry. They are commonly used in transportation hubs like train stations and airports for efficient crowd control. Both types play a crucial role in managing traffic flow and enhancing security in transportation facilities.</p></p>
