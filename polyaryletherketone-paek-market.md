<p><p>Polyaryletherketone (PAEK) is a high-performance polymer known for its excellent mechanical, thermal, and chemical properties. It includes three main types: Polyether ether ketone (PEEK), Polyetherketone (PEK), and Polyetherketoneketone (PEKK). PEEK offers high temperature resistance, PEK provides high toughness, and PEKK combines the benefits of both. These materials are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, medical, and electronics due to their exceptional properties, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and reliability.</p></p>
