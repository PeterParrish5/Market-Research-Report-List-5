There are two main types of bone densitometry scanners on the market: X-ray and ultrasonic. X-ray scanners use low levels of radiation to produce images of bones and measure bone mineral density. Both types of scanners are valuable tools in diagnosing osteoporosis and monitoring bone health.</p></p>
