<p><p>Inert gas filled thermometers are categorized into two main types - Rigid Stem Type and Capillary Type. Rigid stem thermometers have a fixed stem that cannot be bent, making them suitable for measuring temperatures in harsh environments. On the other hand, capillary type thermometers have a flexible capillary tube that allows for measurements in hard-to-reach areas. Both types use inert gas to transmit temperature changes, providing accurate readings for various applications in industries like HVAC, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
