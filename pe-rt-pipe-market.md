<p><p>PE-RT pipe market types refer to different grades of polyethylene with raised temperature resistance that are often used in various applications. Ethylene-Octene, Ethylene-Hexene, and Ethylene-Butene are three common types that differ in the comonomer used during the polymerization process, affecting the final properties of the pipe. Ethylene-Octene offers better stress crack resistance, Ethylene-Hexene provides improved temperature resistance, and Ethylene-Butene offers a balance between the two. Each type caters to specific needs in different industries and applications.</p></p>
