<p><strong>The Hot Runner for Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Gate</li><li>Valve Gate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot runner systems for packaging come in two main types: open gate and valve gate. In open gate systems, plastic material flows continuously through the nozzle into the mold cavity without any interruptions. This results in faster cycle times and reduced material waste. Valve gate systems, on the other hand, control the flow of plastic material using a valve mechanism. This ensures more precise control over the injection process, leading to higher quality parts with minimal gate vestige. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the packaging application.</p></p>
