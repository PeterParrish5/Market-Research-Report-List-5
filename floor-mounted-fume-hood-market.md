<p><p>Floor-mounted fume hoods are categorized into three types based on their exhaust system: Top Exhaust Type, Bottom Exhaust Type, and Top and Bottom Exhaust Type. Top Exhaust Type fume hoods are designed to capture and remove contaminants through an exhaust duct located at the top of the hood. Bottom Exhaust Type fume hoods have the exhaust duct at the bottom of the hood to remove contaminants. Top and Bottom Exhaust Type fume hoods have both top and bottom exhaust ducts for enhanced containment of hazardous fumes and vapors.</p></p>
