<p><p>Autologous Fat Grafting market consists of various types of products used for transferring a patient's own fat from one area of the body to another. Integrated Fat Transfer Systems combine the harvesting and processing of fat in one device. Aspiration and Harvesting Systems are used to collect fat from the body. Liposuction Systems remove excess fat for grafting. Fat Processing Systems purify and prepare the fat for injection. De-Epithelialization Devices remove epithelial tissue from the fat before transfer. Each type serves a specific function in the fat grafting process.</p></p>
