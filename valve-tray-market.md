<p><strong>The Valve Tray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Mobile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Valve trays are commonly used in distillation columns to improve efficiency by evenly distributing liquid and vapor flow. There are two main types of valve trays: fixed and mobile. Fixed valve trays are stationary and remain in a fixed position within the column, providing consistent liquid and vapor distribution. Mobile valve trays, on the other hand, are movable and can adjust their position within the column to optimize performance based on changing operating conditions. Both types of valve trays play a crucial role in enhancing the overall efficiency of distillation processes.</p></p>
