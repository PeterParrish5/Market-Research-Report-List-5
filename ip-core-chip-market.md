<p><p>Soft core IP refers to FPGA-based IP cores that are programmed by the user and offer flexibility in terms of customization. These cores are typically less expensive and quicker to update than hard core IP, making them suitable for prototyping and low-volume production. Hard core IP, on the other hand, is fixed in hardware and cannot be reprogrammed. These cores provide high performance and are ideal for high-volume production applications where stability and reliability are crucial.</p></p>
