<p><strong>The Thermal Spray Coating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flame Spray</li><li>Electric Arc Wire Spray</li><li>Plasma Spray</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal spray coating systems are classified into different types based on the method of coating application. Flame spray involves using a fuel gas and oxygen to create a high-velocity flame to melt the coating material. Electric arc wire spray uses an electric arc to melt the wire feedstock. Plasma spray uses a plasma arc to heat and accelerate the coating material. Other types of thermal spray systems include high-velocity oxy-fuel (HVOF) and cold spray. Each type offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
