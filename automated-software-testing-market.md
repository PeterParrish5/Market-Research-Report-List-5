<p><p>Automated Software Testing Market consists of two main types: Static Testing and Dynamic Testing. Static Testing involves examining the code, requirements, and design documents to find defects without executing the program. Dynamic Testing, on the other hand, involves running tests on the software to evaluate its behavior. Static Testing helps identify issues early in the development lifecycle, while Dynamic Testing evaluates the software's functionality. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of software products in today's fast-paced market.</p></p>
