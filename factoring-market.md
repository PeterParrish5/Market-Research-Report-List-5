<p><p>Factoring market types include domestic factoring and international factoring. Domestic factoring involves the buying and selling of accounts receivable within a single country, providing businesses with cash flow by advancing funds for their unpaid invoices. International factoring expands this practice across borders, enabling companies to access working capital for their foreign trade transactions. Both types of factoring help businesses manage their cash flow and mitigate risks associated with late payments, ultimately supporting their growth and success in the global marketplace.</p></p>
