<p><p>The TEM imaging and spectroscopy equipment market can be divided into different types based on their functionalities. These types include EELS (Electron Energy Loss Spectroscopy), EFTEM (Energy-filtered TEM), and others. EELS is used to analyze the chemical composition of a sample by measuring the energy loss of electrons as they interact with the sample. EFTEM allows for improved contrast and sensitivity in imaging by filtering out unwanted signals. Other types of equipment may offer additional capabilities and features for specialized applications in TEM analysis.</p></p>
