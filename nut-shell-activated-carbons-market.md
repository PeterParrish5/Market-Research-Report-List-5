<p><p>Nut shell activated carbons are commonly sold in two primary forms: granule and powder. Granule activated carbon typically has larger particle sizes, making it ideal for applications where a slower flow rate is desired. It is commonly used in gas phase applications such as air purification and solvent recovery. Powder activated carbon, on the other hand, has smaller particle sizes and is more commonly used in liquid phase applications such as water treatment and food processing where a faster uptake of contaminants is required.</p></p>
