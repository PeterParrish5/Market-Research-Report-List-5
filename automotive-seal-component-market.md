<p><p>The automotive seal component market includes different types of materials such as EPDM, NR, and SBR. EPDM, known for its excellent weather resistance and chemical stability, is commonly used in automotive seals for durability. NR, also known as natural rubber, offers good resilience and elasticity, making it suitable for seals that require flexibility. SBR, or styrene-butadiene rubber, is preferred for its cost-effectiveness and resistance to abrasion. Each material has unique properties that cater to a variety of automotive sealing applications.</p></p>
