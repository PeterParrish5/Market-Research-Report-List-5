Radioimmunoassay involves using antibodies labeled with a radioactive substance to detect PSA levels in the blood, while Enzyme Immunoassay uses enzymes to measure PSA levels. Both methods are widely used in the market for PSA testing, providing accurate and reliable results for the early detection of prostate cancer.</p></p>
