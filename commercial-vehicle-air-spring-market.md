<p><p>The commercial vehicle air spring market is divided into three types: convoluted type, sleeve type, and rolling lobe type. Convoluted type air springs are characterized by their bellows-like shape, providing high load capacity and durability. Sleeve type air springs are compact and easy to install, making them suitable for a wide range of vehicles. Rolling lobe type air springs offer a combination of load capacity, durability, and ride comfort due to their unique design. Each type caters to different vehicle requirements and preferences in the commercial vehicle industry.</p></p>
