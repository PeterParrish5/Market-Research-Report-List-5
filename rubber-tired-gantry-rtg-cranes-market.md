<p><p>Rubber Tired Gantry (RTG) Cranes are commonly available in two market types: 8-wheeler and 16-wheeler. 8-wheeler RTG cranes have four wheels on each side, providing a total of eight wheels for efficient movement and stability. On the other hand, 16-wheeler RTG cranes have eight wheels on each side, totaling to sixteen wheels for heavier lifting capacities and increased stability. Both types offer versatility in handling containers at ports and terminals, but 16-wheeler RTG cranes are typically used for larger and heavier operations.</p></p>
