<p><p>Immuno-oncology drugs are categorized into various types such as immune checkpoint inhibitors, immune system modulators, cancer vaccines, oncolytic virus, and others. Immune checkpoint inhibitors help the immune system recognize and attack cancer cells. Immune system modulators enhance the body's immune response against tumors. Cancer vaccines stimulate the immune system to target cancer cells. Oncolytic virus therapy uses viruses to kill cancer cells. Other types of immuno-oncology drugs include monoclonal antibodies and adoptive cell therapy.</p></p>
