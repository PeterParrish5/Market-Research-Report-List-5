<p><p>Facial Motion Capture Systems have three main types: Magnetic Motion Capture, Mechanical Motion Capture, and Optical Motion Capture. Magnetic Motion Capture uses sensors attached to the face to track movement. Mechanical Motion Capture involves physical markers that reflect light to determine position. Optical Motion Capture uses cameras to capture facial movements. Each type has its own advantages and limitations in terms of accuracy, cost, and ease of use. Customers can choose the right system based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
