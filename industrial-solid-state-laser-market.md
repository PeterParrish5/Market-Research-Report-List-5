<p><strong>The Industrial Solid State Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial solid state lasers can be categorized into two main types based on their operation: pulse type and continuous type. Pulse type lasers emit high-intensity light pulses in short bursts, making them ideal for applications requiring high peak power, such as materials processing and spectroscopy. Continuous type lasers, on the other hand, emit a steady stream of light, making them suitable for applications requiring a constant output, such as cutting and welding. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
