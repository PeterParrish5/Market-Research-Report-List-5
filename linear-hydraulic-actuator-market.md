<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li><li>Double-Rod</li><li>Rodless</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Linear Hydraulic Actuator market is classified into different types based on their functionality and design. Single-Acting actuators use hydraulic pressure to extend the rod, while a separate mechanism is used to retract it. Double-Acting actuators use hydraulic pressure for both extending and retracting the rod. Double-Rod actuators have rods on both sides of the piston for increased stability. Rodless actuators feature a piston connected to a carriage instead of a rod. Other types include telescopic actuators and compact actuators with unique features and applications.</p></p>
