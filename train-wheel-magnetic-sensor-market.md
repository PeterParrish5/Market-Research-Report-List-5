<p><p>Passive railway wheel sensors are designed to detect the presence of a train wheel through magnetic fields without requiring an external power source. They are cost-effective and easy to install. On the other hand, active railway wheel sensors require an external power source to function and provide more precise data on train wheel movements. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of railway operations.</p></p>
