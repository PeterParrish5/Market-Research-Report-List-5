<p><p>Hydraulic Insertion Machines are available in three main market types: Manual, Semi-automatic, and Fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to manually load and unload parts, making them suitable for small-scale production. Semi-automatic machines have partially automated functions, such as automatic insertion but manual part loading. Fully automatic machines offer complete automation, from part loading to insertion and unloading, making them ideal for high-volume production. Each market type caters to different levels of production needs and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
