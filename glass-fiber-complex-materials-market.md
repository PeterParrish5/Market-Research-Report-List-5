<p><p>Glass fiber complex materials market can be categorized into two main types - thermoset composites and thermoplastic composites. Thermoset composites are materials that are formed by curing under heat or pressure, resulting in a permanent solid structure. Thermoplastic composites, on the other hand, are materials that can be molded and reshaped when heated, making them more flexible in terms of design and manufacturing processes. Both types of materials offer unique properties and benefits for various applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
