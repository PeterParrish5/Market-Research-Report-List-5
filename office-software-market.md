<p><p>Office software is available in two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise software is installed locally on a company's server and devices, requiring maintenance and updates to be managed internally. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet, allowing for easy scalability and remote accessibility. While On-Premise software offers more control and security, Cloud-based software offers flexibility and cost-effectiveness.
