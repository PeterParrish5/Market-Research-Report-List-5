<p><p>The Software-Defined Satellite market consists of three main types based on the Earth's orbit: Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), Low Earth Orbit (LEO), and Geosynchronous Earth Orbit (GEO). MEO satellites are positioned at an intermediate altitude, providing global coverage and low latency. LEO satellites are placed closer to the Earth, offering high speed and low latency for data transmission. GEO satellites are positioned at a fixed point above the equator, focusing on providing continuous coverage for communication and broadcasting services.</p></p>
