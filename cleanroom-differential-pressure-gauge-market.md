<p><p>Cleanroom Differential Pressure Gauges are used to monitor the pressure difference between cleanrooms and surrounding areas to ensure a controlled environment. There are two main types: Mechanical Differential Pressure Gauges, which use mechanical components to measure pressure, and Digital Differential Pressure Gauges, which use electronic sensors and display pressure readings digitally. Mechanical gauges are typically more cost-effective and durable, while digital gauges offer accuracy and easy reading. Both types are essential for maintaining proper cleanroom conditions in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, semiconductor manufacturing, and biotechnology.</p></p>
