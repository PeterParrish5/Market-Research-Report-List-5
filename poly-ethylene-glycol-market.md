<p><p>Poly Ethylene Glycol (PEG) Market Types can be classified into Type I and Type II based on their molecular weight. Type I PEGs generally have a lower molecular weight, making them more soluble in organic solvents and water. They are commonly used in pharmaceuticals, personal care products, and industrial applications. On the other hand, Type II PEGs have a higher molecular weight, offering improved lubricating and moisturizing properties. They are often used in food, cosmetics, and drug formulation industries. Both Type I and Type II PEGs have their own unique applications and benefits.</p></p>
