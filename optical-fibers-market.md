<p><strong>The Optical Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optics</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fibers are divided into two main types: multi-mode fiber optics and single-mode fiber optics. Multi-mode fiber optics are designed to carry multiple light rays simultaneously, making them ideal for short-distance communication within buildings or data centers. On the other hand, single-mode fiber optics are built to carry a single light ray, offering higher speeds and longer distances, making them suitable for long-haul communication networks such as telecommunication infrastructure.Both types of fibers play a crucial role in enabling high-speed data transmission in various industries.</p></p>
