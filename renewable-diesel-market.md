Lipids-based renewable diesel is derived from sources like vegetable oils, animal fats, and used cooking oil. Cellulosic biomass-based renewable diesel is made from non-edible parts of plants such as agricultural residues and wood waste. Both types of renewable diesel offer sustainable alternatives to traditional petroleum diesel, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and dependence on fossil fuels.</p></p>
