<p><p>Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLDs) are available in two main types in the market: Surface Mount Technology/Surface Mount Device (SMT/SMD) CPLD and Through Hole CPLD. SMT/SMD CPLDs are mounted directly onto the surface of a PCB, offering a smaller footprint and better space utilization. Through Hole CPLDs are inserted through holes on the PCB, providing a stronger connection but taking up more space. Both types of CPLDs offer powerful programmable logic capabilities for various electronic applications.</p></p>
