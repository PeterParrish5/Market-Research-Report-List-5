<p><p>Spring testing systems are designed to measure and analyze the physical properties of different types of springs. Extension spring testing systems are used to test the tensile strength and elongation of extension springs. Compression spring testing systems measure the compressive strength and deflection of compression springs. Torsion spring testing systems evaluate the torque and angular deflection of torsion springs. Each type of spring testing system is specific to the unique characteristics and requirements of the corresponding spring type to ensure accurate and reliable testing results.</p></p>
