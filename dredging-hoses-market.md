<p><p>Dredging hoses market can be classified into two types: Floating Dredging Hoses and Non-Floating Dredging Hoses. Floating dredging hoses are designed to float on the water's surface, making them ideal for use in deep-sea dredging projects. On the other hand, non-floating dredging hoses are more commonly used in shallower waters and are generally more economical. Both types of hoses are essential for transporting materials such as sediment, slurry, and debris during dredging operations.</p></p>
