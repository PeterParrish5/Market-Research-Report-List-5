<p><p>Confined Space Entry Kits are used to safely enter and work in tight or enclosed spaces. There are two main types of entry kits: Vertical Entry Kits and Side Entry Kits. Vertical Entry Kits are designed for accessing confined spaces with a top entry point, such as tanks or manholes. Side Entry Kits, on the other hand, are used for spaces with a side entry point, like tunnels or pipelines. Both types of kits include equipment such as harnesses, lifelines, ventilation systems, and communication devices for ensuring worker safety in confined spaces.</p></p>
