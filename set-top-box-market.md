<p><p>Set-Top-Box market types include Cable, Satellite, DTT, IP, and OTT. Cable STBs receive signals through coaxial cables from cable operators, while Satellite STBs receive signals via satellite dishes. DTT STBs receive signals through digital terrestrial television transmission. IP STBs utilize internet protocol to deliver content over an IP network. OTT STBs stream content over the internet without the need for traditional cable or satellite services. Each type of STB caters to different broadcasting methods and consumer preferences.</p></p>
