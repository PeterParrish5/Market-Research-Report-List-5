<p><p>Laser ophthalmoscopes are advanced imaging devices used in eye examinations. Scanning laser ophthalmoscopes utilize a laser beam to scan the retina, providing detailed images of the back of the eye. On the other hand, confocal scanning laser ophthalmoscopes use a specialized aperture to eliminate out-of-focus light, resulting in clearer images and better resolution. Both types of ophthalmoscopes are essential tools for diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions, providing valuable insights for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
