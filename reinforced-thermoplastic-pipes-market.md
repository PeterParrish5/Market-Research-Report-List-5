<p><p>Reinforced Thermoplastic Pipes (RTP) are widely used in various industries for their durability and corrosion resistance. Non-metallic RTPs are composed of layers of thermoplastic and fiber reinforcement, offering lightweight and flexible alternatives to traditional metal pipes. On the other hand, Metallic RTPs are constructed with a layer of thermoplastic and a metallic layer for added strength and conductivity. Both types of RTPs cater to different market needs, with non-metallic RTPs being preferred for their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation, while metallic RTPs are chosen for applications requiring higher pressure and temperature resistance.</p></p>
