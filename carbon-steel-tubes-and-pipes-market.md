<p><p>Carbon steel tubes and pipes market consists of two main types - welded tubes and pipes, and seamless tubes and pipes. Welded tubes and pipes are made by welding steel sheets together to form a tube, while seamless tubes and pipes are made by piercing a solid billet of steel to create a seamless tube. Both types have their own advantages and applications, with welded tubes and pipes being more cost-effective and seamless tubes and pipes being stronger and more reliable.</p></p>
