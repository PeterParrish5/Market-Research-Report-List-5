<p><p>Automotive washer pumps are classified into two main types: mono pump and dual pump. Mono pump systems consist of a single pump that delivers washer fluid to both the front and rear windshield. On the other hand, dual pump systems have two separate pumps, with one pump dedicated to the front windshield and the other pump for the rear windshield. Mono pump systems are typically more cost-effective, while dual pump systems provide better efficiency and performance in delivering washer fluid to both windshields simultaneously.</p></p>
