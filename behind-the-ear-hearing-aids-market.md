<p><p>Behind-the-ear hearing aids market is divided into two types: analog hearing aids and digital hearing aids. Analog hearing aids amplify sound waves and convert them into electrical signals, then into amplified sound. They are less expensive but offer limited customization. Digital hearing aids convert sound waves into digital signals for more precise customization to individual hearing needs. They also provide advanced features like noise reduction and feedback controls. These advancements make digital hearing aids more popular in the market compared to analog hearing aids.</p></p>
