<p><p>An Optical Pyrometer is a device used for measuring high temperatures in industrial processes without the need for contact with the object being measured. It works based on the principle of measuring the amount of light emitted by the object and determining its temperature. Optical pyrometers are commonly used in industries such as metal processing, ceramics, glass manufacturing, and heat treatment.</p><p>The Optical Pyrometer Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for accurate temperature measurement in various industrial applications.
