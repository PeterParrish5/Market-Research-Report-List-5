<p><p>Solar pumps are classified into two main types: submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are installed below the water surface and are ideal for deep wells or water sources. They are highly efficient and require minimal maintenance. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are installed above ground and are suitable for shallow water sources or irrigation systems. Both types of solar pumps harness solar energy to power water pumping systems, providing a sustainable and eco-friendly solution for water access in remote areas.</p></p>
