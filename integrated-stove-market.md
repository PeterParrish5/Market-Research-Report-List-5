<p><ul><li>Deep Well Type</li><li>Side Suction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated stove market consists of two main types: Deep Well Type and Side Suction Type. Deep Well Type stoves are designed with a deep well structure that creates a more concentrated heat source, ideal for cooking large quantities of food at once. On the other hand, Side Suction Type stoves have built-in side suctions that efficiently draw in air for combustion, resulting in a more fuel-efficient cooking experience. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and cooking needs in the market.</p></p>
