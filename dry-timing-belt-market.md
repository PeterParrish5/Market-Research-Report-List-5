<p><p>Dry timing belt market consists of various types of components including tensioner, idler pulleys, timing shield/cover, and sprocket. Tensioners are used to maintain proper belt tension, idler pulleys guide the belt around pulley systems, timing shields/covers protect the belt from debris and contamination, and sprockets work with the belt to drive the engine components. Each of these components is crucial for the effective operation and longevity of a timing belt system in various industries including automotive, industrial machinery, and aerospace.</p></p>
